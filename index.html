Golden Tribute

Joining forces to make the world a better place!

Helping Create A More Healthy Environment

Golden Tribute is a organization created by Shamika Fox,a 9th grade student at Parkway Center City to ensure unity amongst a community of people.

Teaching People How To Love The Skin Their in

"Growing up as a child, I was very insecure about how dark I was, I didn't like the idea that no one would like me because I was darker. Not realizing that the pigmentation of my skin was a gift from the sun who loved me oh so much. I want to promote self love so that Children everywhere could become comfortable with theirselves and their surroundings. This program enables people to interact with others about personal problems so that they could be heard even when they believe no one else will listen. If I do not promote Black power, self love, encouragment, and unity, who else will? "-Shamika Fox

Live, Love, Laugh .

"I realized that there are common sterotypes about black people; my main goal would be to show that there is more to people of color than the violence, division between economic structures, and how we might carry ourselves. I believe we have more to us, we have the ability to help people, we should be able to come together and show others that we have over come many obstacles, and we will not allow the past to reflect on our future. We will not repeat the cycle, but we will start our own."-Shamika Fox

Explore The World, For It Is OURS .

"We are thed new generation, which means we will have the power and authority, if I don't promote black power who will? It's not to degrade others of their cultural background but to embrace what many lost, to continue the legacy.-Shamika Fox
