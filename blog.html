Why is that when we are so close we tend to give up?

Success is the only option, we can not make excuses after excuses trying to justify our actions. We ust thrive for the accomplishments of our goals, we can not repeat the cycle of family memebers who did not graduate with Bachelors, Masters, and Doctoral degrees.

I've learned the hard way that success does not fall out of the great blue sky. If you want something you must make it a priority, you will make mistakes, it will be hard, but thats why pencils have erasers.

Excuses are as followed, excuses are the tools for the weak and incompetent which builds bridges to no where and monuments of nothingness. So when we make an excuse we are showing that we are not strong enough to hold on the massive force of work, then we give up.

You have to keep going because miracles happen the very moment you are about to give up.
